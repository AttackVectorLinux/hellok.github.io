## Turn off stack randomization
$ sudo sysctl -w kernel.randomize_va_space=0
add above into "/etc/sysctrl.conf"
## Turn off 0 address space
$ sudo sysctl -w vm.mmap_min_addr=0
Testing NULL dereference attack
